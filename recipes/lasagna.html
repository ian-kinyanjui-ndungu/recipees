Ultimate Low-Carb Zucchini Lasagna

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • 1 ½ large zucchinis, thinly sliced lengthwise
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 ½ cups low-carb marinara sauce
  • 2 teaspoons salt, divided
    • >
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • ½ teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • aluminum foil

DIRECTONS

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch
square baking dish with cooking spray.


step2

Pat dry zucchini slices with a paper towel to remove excess moisture. Set aside.


step3

Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add ground beef; cook until
browned, 5 to 8 minutes.


step4

Add marinara sauce, 1 teaspoon salt, oregano, and pepper; simmer for 10 minutes.
Set aside.


step5

Combine ricotta cheese, egg, 1 teaspoon salt, and nutmeg in a bowl; mix well. Set aside.


step6

Arrange one layer of zucchini slices in the prepared baking dish. Cover with 1/2
of the sauce. Add another layer of zucchini slices. Spread ricotta mixture on top.
Sprinkle with 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Add another layer of zucchini slices;
cover with remaining sauce and top with remaining mozzarella cheese and
Parmesan cheese. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.


step7

Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Remove aluminum foil and bake until top is golden, about 15 minutes more.
